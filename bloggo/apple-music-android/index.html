Apple Music on Android

I use Apple Music on my Android phone. I doesn't have shorts if you scroll a bit too far. It doesn't have twenty million niche mixes for you to explore. It doesn't have 30 million niche playlists to explore. It doesn't have tons of shuffle modes.

It has a focus on Albums. You add an Album to your library, your collection. Spotify often releases a single, then an album. On Apple Music you get one song of the album. When the whole album unlocks, you can listen to everything.

Apple Music supports Lossless audio. In their cheapest plan. Which you can get through Google Payments. I think it's interesting Apple is willing to pay the 15% subscription fee to Google. It might help with their legal battle against Spotify.

Apple Music has a function to cast to Chromecast. It doesn't work well. It stops playing after 10 minutes or so. It's not reliable at all.

You can also use Apple Music on Windows or Linux. Windows has an app with full functionality available in the Microsoft Store. On Linux you'll have to settle for a web version. You can also use Cider.
