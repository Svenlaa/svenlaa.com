Limits of being self-taught

I am noticing things about me that show I don't have any formal education like my colleagues. This can work in my favour. I have more practical experience, my coworkers get surprised by my knowledge of the browser devtools.

We're exploring more fields and patterns, and I don't have experience in any of it. My breath of knowledge is small. I'm decent at fields I'm "specialized" in, but barely know anything beyond that. I don't even know where to start. If you have a starting point, it's easier to branch out and grow.
