Sony in Pink

Sony has just announced "smoky pink" versions of their flagship earbuds (WF-1000XM5) and headphones (WH-1000XM5). It's an understated pink. It looks quite gray. The advert has members of the KPOP Girl Group Le Sserafim wearing them. They launch on the 30th of September 2024. They're available for preorder. I enjoy the music of the KPOP group.

Notably, a marketing video for the headphones on the Dutch retailer bol.com claims the headphones are the best earbuds with noise cancelling. That's the wrong product category. I sent them an email. They asked for details. I responded with details. Sony haven't responded yet.
